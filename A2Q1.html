Assassin's Creed

Protagonists Gameplay
  1. Story
  1. Desmond Miles
    • Desmond Miles, a bartender who is a descendant of several lines of prominent Assassins
    • though raised as an Assassin, he left his nomadic family to seek out a more common lifestyle
    • Desmond is forced into the "Animus" and is revealed to be subject 17
  2. Later Story
    • Desmond is later rescued by a small team of modern-day Assassins
    • Desmond gains some of the Assassin skills of his predecessors at the cost of living with multiple sets of memories and personalities in his mind
    • Within the Animus, Desmond explores the memories of a number of Assassins
  1. Game Present
  1. Animus
    • The bulk of the game is played as Desmond experiences the memories of his ancestors through an advanced device called the Animus
    • This provides a means of a diegetic interface, showing Desmond's ancestor's health, equipment, goals
    • The Animus is based on the player controlling the assassin to maintain the synchronization between Desmond and his ancestor's memories
  2. While Playing
    • the games are generally presented as third-person in an open world
    • focusing on stealth and free-running
    • The games use a mission structure to follow the main story
